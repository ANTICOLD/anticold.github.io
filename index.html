About

I received my Ph.D. at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Evangeline F.Y. Young, in 2022. Previously, I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Sichuan University. My current research interests include Placement and Routing (PnR), VLSI Physical Design, Combinatorial Optimization and Machine learning.
